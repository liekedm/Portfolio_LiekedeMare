Coffee and Thee Game

Design for here and now

The Drink Bomb is a game for student houses that lets you decide who needs to get up and make drinks for the whole house. With the Drink Bomb you can indicate what you want to drink (coffee, tea or water) and see what others want. When the game starts lights appear on one of the boxes. By smashing the button the lights transfer to someone else. After a certain time the lights turn red and canâ€™t be transferred anymore. The person with the red lights need to get drinks.
